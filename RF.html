Pathogenesis & Molecular Mechanisms

Molecular Mimicry

Shared epitopes between Group A Streptococcus (GAS) M-protein and human cardiac myosin trigger autoreactive T-cell–mediated valve damage.

Key Pathways: VCAM-1–dependent granulomatous inflammation, IL-17/Th17 activation (rs2275913-A allele), and M1-macrophage–driven fibrosis via VLA4/VCAM-1.

Early RHD Biomarkers

  • Elevated IgG2 autoantibodies against N-acetyl-β-D-glucosamine (GlcNAc)
  • Epigenetic TNF-α/IL-6–triggered pro-fibrotic gene expression

Diagnosis & Updated Criteria

2015 Revised Jones Criteria – Key Changes

  • Echocardiography = gold standard for carditis (≥2 views MR, jet ≥2 cm, Vmax >3 m/s)
  • Subclinical carditis now a major manifestation
  • Risk-stratified thresholds (low vs moderate-high incidence regions)

Advances in Screening & AI

Handheld + AI Echocardiography

  • AUC 0.93 (support-vector machines) and 0.84 (deep learning)
  • Community nurses: 89 % accuracy after 2-month training (Fiji)
  • WHF 2023 Staging A–D continuum replaces “borderline/definite” labels

Secondary Prophylaxis

IM Benzathine Penicillin G (BPG)

  • Adherence ≥80 % → markedly ↓ ARF recurrence
  • SMS reminders improve compliance
  • Lidocaine mix reduces injection pain; oral-penicillin trials ongoing (GOALIE)

Indian Scenario

  • Dual burden: declining ARF in some states, rising Kawasaki disease in urban centres (e.g., Chandigarh)
  • Over 100 000 annual RHD deaths
  • POCUS & community-based penicillin programs emerging
